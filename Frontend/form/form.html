Writing a good grievance

  • Clearly state the issue in a brief and specific manner.
  • Provide important information like course name, date, and names of involved parties.
  • Stick to the facts and avoid emotional language.
  • Clearly state what you're hoping to achieve as a resolution.
  • Add “tags” which help surface your question.
  • Review your question and post it to the site.
